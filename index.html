About us

Kota Nayak Foundation “Service of oppressed humanity is service of the almighty” Established in 1964, Kota Nayak Foundation is the Top Nonprofit Trust headquartered in Kota, Udupi, Karnataka, India

The organization started 5 decades ago with a vision to serve the needy and cure the helpless patients through free corrective surgeries with a will to fight a battle with Polio and other related birth disabilities. An internationally renowned center with more than 60 specialized hospitals, 2200+ beds, food for 6500+ people every day, and more than 5427850 free of cost corrective surgeries; the Foundation has become more like a family and home to all the patients and their visitors.
